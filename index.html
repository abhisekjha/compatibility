Relationship Compatibility Test

Evaluate your compatibility and assess the potential success of your relationship.

How to Use This Checklist

For each of the 25 attributes below, rate your compatibility on a scale of 1 to 4:

  • 1 (Low Compatibility): Significant differences that could cause conflict.
  • 2 (Moderate Compatibility): Some differences, but manageable with effort.
  • 3 (High Compatibility): Generally aligned with minor differences.
  • 4 (Very High Compatibility): Strong alignment and mutual understanding.

After rating each attribute, click the "Calculate Score" button to assess the potential success of your relationship.
